The Pig Game

The Pig game is simple to learn as the rules are simple to explain.


  • First the game is for two players. Each player rolls a single die as many times as he/she wishes. The result of each roll is added to his/her ROUND score.
  • If the player rolls a 1 he loses ROUND score and the turn passes to the next player
  • Additionally, if a player rolls a double six, he/she loses both his round and Global score.
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his "Global" score. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • The first player to reach 100 points in their "Global" score wins the game.
